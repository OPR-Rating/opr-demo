Getting started

Put OPR on your phone — no app store needed.

OPR isn't a download. It's a website you can add to your home screen, so it looks and opens just like an app.

✓ Nothing is installed on your phone

OPR is simply a website — opening it is as safe as opening your bank's site or a news page. There's no app to download, nothing from the Play Store or App Store, and nothing installed onto your phone. Adding it to your home screen just gives the website its own icon, so you can reach it in one tap. That's all it is.

Add it in a few taps

Pick your phone type below. It takes about fifteen seconds.

Android

Using Chrome

Open your club's OPR link in Chrome.

Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots) in the top-right corner.

Tap "Add to Home screen" (it may say "Install app").

Tap Add to confirm. The OPR icon appears on your home screen.

✨ Now just tap the OPR icon any time — it opens full-screen, like any app.

iPhone

Using Safari

Open your club's OPR link in Safari.

Tap the Share button (the square with an up-arrow) at the bottom of the screen.

Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".

Tap Add in the top corner. The OPR icon appears on your home screen.

✨ Now just tap the OPR icon any time — it opens full-screen, like any app.

? Why isn't OPR in the Play Store?

Because it doesn't need to be — and that's actually safer for you. App-store apps install software onto your phone and need updating through the store. OPR is a website, so it's always the latest version the moment you open it, with nothing to install and nothing to keep updated. Adding it to your home screen gives you the convenience of an app with none of the download worries.

Ready

That's it — you're set.

Tap your OPR icon, enter your scores, and watch your rating and your club ladder come to life.
