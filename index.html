Why OPR is different

Built for your club —
not a faceless global pool.

Most rating apps drop your members into one anonymous worldwide ladder. OPR gives each club its own private space, its own standings, and a rating engine you can actually explain.

The club owns it

Your members, your ladders, your data — in your own space. Not held on a platform you don't control, behind numbers you can't see.

Padel is doubles

You and your regular partner get a standing within your own club — known by name, rising and falling together. Built for pairs from the ground up.

Fair & transparent

The rating rewards how well you played — sets, games and the strength of who you faced — and the logic is explained openly. No black box.

Run by you

A full tournament — group rounds, semis and a final — in a single afternoon. Or a season-long ladder. No outside help, no technical know-how.

A standing of your own

Two players.
One rating. Your club.

On a global app you're one more anonymous number. With OPR, you and your partner have a place in your community — a story that rises and falls match by match, known by name at the club you actually play at.

For the club, that becomes part of what membership means: a reason to join, a reason to stay, and something genuinely its own.

What your club runs

Three things, one platform.

Everything a club needs to keep members playing, climbing and coming back — between events and during them.

01 · Ratings & form

A living number for every player

One rating that moves with every match, plus a form line showing who's hot right now. Scores how you played, not just whether you won.

02 · The club ladder

Climb by challenging upward

A living leaderboard of pairs. Beat a team above you, take their spot. The club's heartbeat between tournaments — and a reason to keep showing up.

03 · Tournaments

A full event in an afternoon

Fair banded pairings, Swiss-style rounds, divisions for big fields, semis and a final — and a champion crowned automatically. No outside help.
