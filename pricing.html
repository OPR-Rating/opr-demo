Talk to us about your club

Priced in your currency, to suit your club

Wherever your club is — Europe, southern Africa, the Gulf — we price in your own currency and set the fee to fit how your club is funded. Talk to us and we'll make it simple.

Why flat per club, not per player?

Because a club shouldn't be punished for growing. Per-player apps charge you more every time a member joins — so success costs you money. OPR is the opposite: one price, the whole club, however many members play. It's the fairest model for the people actually running the game.

Add-ons, when you want them

Optional extras — never required, available as your club grows.

  • OPR club terminal — a ready-to-go tablet for the clubhouse, so members tap in scores as they leave.
  • Club sections — separate ladders for groups within your club (e.g. a Tuesday social), all under one roof.
  • Tournament-day support — a hand on the day for your big events.
